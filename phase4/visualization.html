Phase 4 — Hybrid Activation Precision: Pareto Analysis

All weights are trit. Activation precision varies per component per layer.

Key finding: BitNet a4.8 (ffn_down=int8, rest=int4) is Pareto-optimal. It achieves lower quality degradation than uniform int4 at nearly the same performance. The mechanism: FFN down inputs have massive outliers (SmoothQuant, BitNet a4.8); keeping only this component at int8 captures ~49% of the quality benefit of full int8.
